The Early Years Gap (Age 3-5)

Critical Difference

Ontario

Age 4 Junior Kindergarten (JK)

Age 3 is Private Daycare. The public system formally invites children at Age 4. It is play-based and optional but widely attended.

Earliest Start

Dubai (UK)

Age 3 FS1 (Foundation Stage 1)

Formal Schooling. Children wear uniforms and attend "Big School" campus. Focus on early literacy and numeracy begins gently but earlier.

Australia

Age 5 Prep / Foundation

Age 3-4 is Preschool. Often separate centres (Kindergartens). State primary school typically starts at Age 5.

University Acceptance Index

Perception of diploma rigour by admissions officers in different regions.

Ontario (OSSD)

Pros: Modular system allows mark maximization. Best for Canadian Universities. Flexible "Top 6" average calculation.

Global unis may view it as less rigorous than IB without AP courses.

Dubai (IB Diploma)

Pros: The "Gold Standard". Advanced credit in US. Highly respected in UK. Develops massive resilience.

Extremely high workload. 45-point scale is unforgiving.

Australia (ATAR)

Pros: Transparent state-wide ranking. Strong pathways to AU/Asian universities.

"Scaling" system can punish students in "easier" subjects.
